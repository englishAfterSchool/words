--ask polite questions
Polite indirect questions:
	I'd like to know ...
	Could you tell me ...?
	May I ask ... ?
	Do you happen to know ... ?
Interrupting politely:
	Excuse me, but ...
	May I ask a question?
	Could I (please) ask ... ?
	
--ask questions
Open question:
	How ... ?
	Why ... ? - why do you like smth?
	For what reason ... ? - For what reason do you do smth.
Asking for explanations:
	What exactly do you mean by ... ? - What exactly do you mean by "abstract mind" ?
	Could you explain a bit further ... ?
	Would you like to add to your comments about ... ?
