мнение (how you give opinion):
1. strongly opinion
	I am convinced that ...
	It's really clear that ...
	It's obvious that ...
2. neutral
	I think that ...
	I believe that ...
3. weak
	It's seems to me that ...
	I would say that ...
4. expressing concession
	Although ...	- Although nuclear energy is clear, I wonder if it really safe.
	In spite of the fact that ... - In spite of the fact that it's dangerous, a lot of young people still smoke.
